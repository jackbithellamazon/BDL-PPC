First-time Supabase setup
Step 1 — Go to supabase.com and create a free project
Step 2 — In your project, open SQL Editor and run the schema (click "Copy SQL to clipboard" above, paste, run)
Step 3 — Go to Settings → API. Copy your Project URL and anon/public key into the fields above
Step 4 — Click "Save & Connect" — the status should turn green
Step 5 — Click "Force full sync" to push all your existing data to Supabase
